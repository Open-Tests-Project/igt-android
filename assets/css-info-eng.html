CSV file

At the end of the test (by accessing the menu in the second page of charts), you can save a Csv file.
At any time, you can view some charts, uploading the Csv file saved in this web page (better if you use a tablet or a computer):
www.simonesacchi.com/igt-charts/#eng

The data saved are:
  1. the participant's name
  2. the trial number
  3. the selected deck
  4. the reward
  5. the penalty
  6. the net gain (reward - penalty)
  7. the total earned
  8. the response time (the interval in milliseconds between trials)
  9. the pressure* put on display (if supported)
  10. the pressure style** (if supported)
  11. the touch speed***
  12. the time
  13. the date

* for each touch, the pressure can be measured in two moments: when the finger begins the contact with the screen and when the finger stops the contact. The data saved is the average of these two values.

** this is an experimental measure, each touch can take only three values:

  • LOW_HIGH: when the initial pressure is less than the final pressure
  • HIGH_LOW: when the initial pressure is greater than the final pressure
  • EQUAL: when the two pressures are equal

*** for how many milliseconds the finger is in contact with the display
